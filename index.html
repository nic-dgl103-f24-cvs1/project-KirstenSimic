About Our Markets

We have 2 markets to serve you, a year-round market on Saturday in Courtenay and a seasonal market in Cumberland on Sunday. All of our markets have live music, seating areas, market money, accessible parking and plenty of parking. Note that for health and safety reasons dogs (and pets), except service dogs, are not permitted at the market.

Looking for a vendor? Need a bag? Need Market Tokens? Lost something? Have a suggestion? Please let us know by chatting with us in person at the Market Information Tent or on our website or social media channels.
